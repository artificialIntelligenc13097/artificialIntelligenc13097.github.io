What is — Artificial intelligence?

From SIRI to self-driving cars, artificial intelligence (AI) is progressing rapidly. While science fiction often portrays AI as robots with human-like characteristics, AI can encompass anything from Google’s search algorithms to IBM’s Watson to autonomous weapons.

Data & AI

Powered by advanced analytics, machine learning, AI, and high performance computing (HPC) capabilities, the data revolution is redefining how products are designed, manufactured, and sold.

Artificial intelligence

Artificial intelligence today is properly known as narrow AI (or weak AI), in that it is designed to perform a narrow task (e.g. only facial recognition or only internet searches or only driving a car).

Data & AI

However, the long-term goal of many researchers is to create general AI (AGI or strong AI).

Artificial intelligence

While narrow AI may outperform humans at whatever its specific task is, like playing chess or solving equations, AGI would outperform humans at nearly every cognitive task.

How Does —Artificial Intelligence Work?

1

Turing's paper "Computing Machinery and Intelligence" (1950), and it's subsequent Turing Test, established the fundamental goal and vision of artificial intelligence.

2

At it's core, AI is the branch of computer science that aims to answer Turing's question in the affirmative.

3

It is the endeavor to replicate or simulate human intelligence in machines.
